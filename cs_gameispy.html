Revitalising Museum trips for Children and focusing learning through entertainment
iSPY Explore App
Compelling content:
Museums are fascinating places full of weird and wonderful things, archiving brilliance forgotten by the world. The challenge lies in getting children excited by museums and interested in what they are showcasing. With the new generation so engrossed in the digital way of living, it’s difficult to connect them to anything that isn’t new and shiny.

ISpy aims to bridge this gap by guiding children through physical museums through a digital vessel. The app works with museums to guide, inform and entertain children highlighting the certain sections for the best experience through the building.
Short, Concise:
Museums can have as many or as few sections for a fully tailored experience. The experience can last for a few minutes or up to the duration of the visit. After each find, the players are rewarded with information about what they have found so it becomes more than a simple treasure hunt.
Physical and Digital:
Creating an experience through the combination of physical points of interest and digital guidance through an app. After completion, children may get a small reward from the venue further encouraging them to explore museums.

  The iSpy Explore is downloadable from the App store and Google Play
