AR you being served? The Superdrug sales assistant helping inform customers
Superdrug AR App
Compelling content:
Just how do you tell a consumer everything you want to about a product on a small bit of packaging? And how can a store assistant know everything there is to know about every product on the shelfs? Superdrug were looking to answer this to both aid their in-store staff and aid consumers. The idea being using AR to expand the product – simply scanning the product with a smartphone opens up everything from videos to reviews, to direct consultation with a pharmacist.
Short, Concise:
Superdrug were not looking to replace their instore staff, just provide them with the tools to be as effective as possible, to deliver a high level of customer service expected in the retail environment.
Physical and Digital:
A prototype project – whilst the technology isn’t readily available today Superdrug had the foresight to investigate what the future might bring, what might the store of tomorrow bring? And with the dawn of 5G and integrated AR this prototype may very soon be an everyday tool where the physical product is the window to the digital shelf.

  The AR feature was integrated into the Superdrug app.
